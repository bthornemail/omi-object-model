# Metatron

## Role

Metatron is the balanced alternating witness and scribe. The scribe of
apparent difference.

## Constant

```
0xAA55 = 10101010 01010101
```

8 ones, 8 zeros, exactly balanced. Complementary halves (0xAA and 0x55).
Alternating pattern — difference made visible at every step.

## Properties

```
Not the transition itself.
Not the validator.
Not the source of motion.
Not the carrier.

The reference-defining observer at the boundary where change becomes visible.
```

## Rules

```
Metatron witnesses the crossing.
Metatron scribes the visible difference.
Metatron does not validate or accept.
That belongs to Tetragrammatron.
```
