# Glossary

## omi-notation

The canonical notation surface: `omi---imo?O_o`. The folded root
circulation with its readable witness branch.

## omi-lisp

The local declaration surface. Structured declarations using cons pairs,
dot notation, and NULL seeds.

## omi-port

The external/adaptive declaration surface. Handles port declarations
for connecting to external systems.

## Null Ring

The folded null seed `(NULL . NULL)` and its byte-ring interpretation:
`0x00 -> 0x20 -> 0x7F -> 0xFF -> 0x00`. Full witness closure:
`0x20 ^ 0x5F ^ 0x80 ^ 0xFF = 0x00`.

## Gauge Ring

The deterministic normalization ruler. Fixed digital null-byte ring for
algorithmic carry-forward.

## Omicron

The deterministic normalization and closure surface. Fixed digital
null-byte ring. Algorithmic carry-forward. Does not change.

## Omnicron

The resolver and projection surface. Axiomatic resolver surface for
lived device/software/hardware/world resolution. Changes according
to environment.

## Metatron

The balanced alternating witness. `0xAA55` = 8 ones, 8 zeros, exactly
balanced. The scribe of apparent difference. Projection as observational
reference.

## Tetragrammatron

The lawful fold governor. Validates exact nibble and XOR closure
conditions. Fold patterns: `0x3C` (centered), `0x78` (carried/shifted).

## 8-tuple

A pedagogical automata-style scaffold for explaining how OMI notation
may be read as a bounded relation frame. Not canonical structure, not
an authority layer, and not a proof object.
